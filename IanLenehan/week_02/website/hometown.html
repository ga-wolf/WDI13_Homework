Matsumoto was born in Osaka Prefecture in 1965 and spent his early years there before being raised in Tottori Prefecture, nearly 200km away.

Osaka Prefecture is located in the Kansai region on Honshu, the main island of Japan. Osaka, it's capital, is one of the two "urban prefectures" of Japan, the other being Kyoto. Tokyo is a metropolitan prefecture.

Osaka Prefecture was created on June 21, 1868, at the very beginning of the Meiji era. It is neighbour to the prefectures of Hyōgo and Kyoto in the north, Nara in the east and Wakayama in the south. The west is open to Osaka Bay. The Yodo and Yamato Rivers flow through the prefecture.

According to the 2005 Population Census of Japan, Osaka prefecture has a population of 8,817,166.
