Some facts

RMS refuses to have supermarket frequent buyer cards of his own if they have any identifying data, because they are a form of surveillance.

Up until 1998, RMS's office at MIT was also the place of his residence.

RMS likes trains, and in general he would rather take a train for many hours than fly.

RMS does not use a mobile phone as they are tracking and surveillance devices.
