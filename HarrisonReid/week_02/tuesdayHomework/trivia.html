Some more intersting facts about John von Neumann...

  • He was under military security at the time of his death, due to concerns that he might reveal sensitive military information while under heavy sedation.
  • It is thought that he posessed the power of absolute recall - a photographic memory. He was apparently able to recall, word for word, texts that he had read only a single time years before.
  • He was reportedly an awful driver.
  • Though he had been a lifelong agnostic, he converted to Catholicism on his deathbed.
